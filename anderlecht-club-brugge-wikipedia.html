R.S.C. Anderlecht–Club Brugge KV rivalry - Wikipedia

The Anderlecht vs Club Brugge derby is a football rivalry between two Belgian Pro League clubs R.S.C. Anderlecht and Club Brugge KV, the two most successful clubs in Belgium.The rivalry started in the early 1970s as Club Brugge emerged as a major title contender to the dominant Anderlecht. They have contested three Belgium Cup finals with Club Brugge victorious on two of those occasions.

R.S.C. Anderlecht - Wikipedia

After three-second-place finishes in a row, the Purple and Whites secured an easy 18th title in 1984–85, 11 points ahead of Club Brugge. In 1985–86, Anderlecht won the championship again, but this time after a two-legged play-off against Club Brugge. Anderlecht won their 20th championship on the last matchday of the 1986–87 season.

Club Brugge KV - Wikipedia

Club Brugge Koninklijke Voetbalvereniging (Dutch pronunciation: [klʏˈbrʏɣə ˌkoːnɪŋkləkə ˈvudbɑlvəreːnəɣɪŋ]), commonly referred to as just Club Brugge, or Club Bruges commonly in English, is a Belgian professional football club based in Bruges in Belgium. It was founded in 1891 and its home ground is the Jan Breydel Stadium, which has a capacity of 29,062.
