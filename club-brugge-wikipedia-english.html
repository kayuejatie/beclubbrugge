It was founded in 1891 and its home ground is the Jan Breydel Stadium, which has a capacity of 29,062.

Bart Verhaeghe - Wikipedia

Bart Verhaeghe (pronounced [ˈbɑrt fərˈɦaːɣə]), (born in 1965) is a Belgian entrepreneur. He became president of Club Brugge in 2011 and one year later he became the majority shareholder. He is also chairman of project developer UPLACE and former vice-chairman of the Royal Belgian Football Association.He also co-founded the independent think tank Itinera in 2006.
